About Tesla Crafted

Our Mission

Tesla Crafted was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate Tesla owners who wanted to create a comprehensive resource for Tesla accessory reviews, guides, and recommendations.

Our mission is to help Tesla owners find the best accessories and products to enhance their electric vehicle experience. We believe that the right accessories can make Tesla ownership even more enjoyable, convenient, and personalized.

What We Do

At Tesla Crafted, we:

  • Test and review Tesla accessories from various manufacturers
  • Create detailed guides for DIY Tesla modifications and upgrades
  • Share maintenance tips to keep your Tesla in optimal condition
  • Showcase innovative products that enhance the Tesla experience
  • Build a community of Tesla enthusiasts who share ideas and experiences

Our Team

Our team consists of Tesla owners across different models (Model 3, Model Y, Model S, and Model X) who bring diverse perspectives and expertise to our content. From software engineers to automotive enthusiasts, we combine our knowledge to provide you with the most comprehensive and accurate information.

Our Values

  • Honesty: We provide unbiased reviews based on real testing
  • Quality: We only recommend products that meet our high standards
  • Community: We value the input and feedback from our readers
  • Sustainability: We support products that align with Tesla's eco-friendly mission
